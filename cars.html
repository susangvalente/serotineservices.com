About

What is this thing?

Motors and Milkshakes is a free, community car show organized by VJ Valente right here in Grand County, Colorado. No entry fees, no judges, no trophies. Just great cars, good conversation, and cold milkshakes.

We call Granby home, with most of our shows at Debbie's Drive In. Four times each summer, keeping it local and keeping it fun. Every make, model, and year is welcome. Bring your classic, your project car, your daily driver, or just come and look around.

Grand County deserves a car show. This is it.
